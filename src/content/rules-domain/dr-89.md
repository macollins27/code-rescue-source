Canonical-identity resolution is MANDATORY per INV-3 and product-vision.md §7.0 ("Canonical-identity resolution is MANDATORY. If every resolver is unreachable AND the user has not entered their APN manually from their tax bill, the claim is blocked."). The mandate is on the resolver-layer availability as a whole, NOT on any single vendor — Regrid primary per INV-4(b) (ATTOM deferred from v1 implementation), and manual APN entry per C-104(f) together form the resolver abstraction. On total resolver failure (every vendor path errors AND the user has not submitted a manual APN from their tax bill via the C-104(f) fallback flow per INV-3), property creation is BLOCKED with TRPCError({ code: "BAD_REQUEST", message: "Address resolution is temporarily unavailable. Please enter your county APN from your tax bill, or try again later.", cause: { code: "RESOLVER_OUTAGE" } }) per PCI-7 / ERR-8. There is no graceful degradation path — a property without canonical identity (upi) cannot be deduplicated, cannot be lineage-tracked (DR-130), and cannot be claimed.
